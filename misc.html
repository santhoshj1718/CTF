Miscellaneous CTF Tips

General Strategy

  1. Read the challenge description carefully
  2. Identify what's given and what's needed
  3. Break the problem into smaller parts
  4. Look for patterns or repetitions
  5. Automate repetitive tasks

Quick Wins

  • Check for simple encoding (base64, hex)
  • Try common mathematical operations
  • Look for hidden patterns in data
  • Use constraints solvers for logic puzzles
  • Google similar problems
Pro Tip: When stuck:
  • Take a break and come back with fresh eyes
  • Explain the problem to someone else
  • Try solving a simpler version first
  • Look for writeups of similar challenges
